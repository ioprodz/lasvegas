Audio Animations

Audio Chase

Light chases around strip. Speed from bass, color from dominant frequency.

Sparkle Rain

High frequencies spawn bright sparks at random positions. Bass drives a warm background glow.

Energy Bars

Each row is a frequency band with centered energy bars. Smooth attack/decay for fluid motion.

Hybrid

Six blocks alternating Pulse and Chase. Each block has its own phase for varied, layered patterns.

Synesthesia

Melody dot + hybrid blocks. The pitch-tracking dot floats on top of hybrid's classic animations. Fast melody movement reshuffles the layout.

Harmonic V2

Symmetric, cohesive blocks designed for 6x60. Aurora curtains, diamond breathe, rain, gradient sweep, mirror bars, and firefly drift. Mirrored layout for visual balance. Same chords = same look.

Harmonic V3

Counter-motion blocks. Each block's direction is deterministically reversed or forward from the chord fingerprint. Chases, waves, and pulses flow in opposing directions for visual counterpoint.

Harmonic V4

The best of V2 and V3. Beautiful aurora curtains, diamond breathe, rain, gradient sweeps, mirror bars, and firefly drift — all with per-block counter-motion. Mirrored blocks flow in opposite directions. Same chords = same look, but with visual counterpoint.

Harmonic V5

Illusions & organic FX. Fire with rising embers, vortex spirals, lava blobs that merge and split, plasma interference, swimmer dots on Lissajous orbits, and expanding strobe rings. Melody speed drives animation pace; chords drive colors.

Harmonic V6

Pastel Dreams. Soft orbiting particles trace block perimeters, harmonic waves tilt with frequency, bubbles float with treble, ribbons weave with BPM, petals bloom with beat, and clouds drift and blend. All in dreamy pastel tones.

Tidal

No blocks, no particles. The whole strip is one flowing surface. Six rows map to six frequency bands creating luminous strata. Color tides sweep with tempo, melody glows as a traveling light, kicks send ripples outward. Pure smooth gradients.

Drift

Tidal background with soft glowing orbs drifting in smooth Lissajous curves. Note changes spawn orbs at the melody position, kicks burst new ones across the strip. Orbs float gracefully over flowing color strata. Zero flicker.

Fire

Realistic fire simulation across the full grid. Bass and energy inject heat at the bottom, which rises, spreads sideways, and cools. Rendered with a fire palette from black through red, orange, yellow to white hot.

Pac-Man

Pac-Man cruises across the strip eating dots on every beat. Mouth opens and closes to the rhythm. Two ghosts trail behind with wiggling tentacles. Power pellets pulse, dots respawn ahead. Dark blue maze background.

Retro Arcade

Six classic games in chord-driven blocks: Space Invaders, Pong, Snake, Flappy Bird, Nyan Cat, and Mario. Chord progressions shuffle which game plays in each block. Kicks trigger jumps, flaps, and bullets. BPM drives game speed.

Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life on the LED grid. Simulation speed tracks BPM and energy. Kicks spawn classic shapes (gliders, spaceships, R-pentominos). When cells die off, new patterns auto-spawn. Cell colors follow chord changes, aging shifts hue over time.
